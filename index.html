Our Story

Built by One Person. Built for Real.

AOK T-Shirts started from a simple idea: your apparel should mean something. Not a logo slapped on a blank. Not a trend drop designed for the algorithm. Something that connects to who you are and where you come from.

We draw from cultures and disciplines that demand respect — Filipino martial arts, Polynesian heritage, combat sports, and the emerging identity of Web3. These aren't aesthetics we borrowed. They're lived values we wear.

AOK is an independent brand. Everything is print-on-demand — no warehouse, no excess inventory, no waste. Your order is made when you place it.

The name means different things to different people. Art of Kali. Aloha Over Konformity. Age Over Kryptonite. Pick the one that fits. They're all true.

Who We Are

AOK T-Shirts is an independent apparel brand operating at aoktshirts.com. We design and sell print-on-demand apparel across platforms including Etsy and Pinterest. This privacy policy governs data collected through this website and our third-party integrations, including the Pinterest API.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to operate our business:

  • Contact inquiries: Name and email address when you contact us voluntarily via email.
  • Analytics: Standard web server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited, referral source) for site performance and security purposes.
  • Third-party platform data: When you interact with our content on Etsy or Pinterest, those platforms collect data per their own privacy policies. We do not receive personally identifiable information from those interactions unless you initiate contact with us directly.

We do not use tracking pixels, behavioral advertising cookies, or any third-party retargeting scripts on this website.

Pinterest API

AOK T-Shirts uses the Pinterest API to manage and schedule pins related to our product collections on Pinterest. This integration:

  • Accesses only AOK T-Shirts' own Pinterest business account content (boards, pins, analytics)
  • Does not access, store, or process data from Pinterest users who have not explicitly connected their account to our application
  • Is used solely for content publication, scheduling, and basic analytics of our own account performance
  • Does not share Pinterest user data with any third party

Pinterest's own privacy policy governs your use of Pinterest.com and its platform.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to inquiries and customer service requests

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. We do not use your data for advertising targeting beyond our own branded content published to our own social accounts.
