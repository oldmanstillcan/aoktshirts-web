Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2025  ·  Last updated: March 2026

Who We Are

AOK T-Shirts is an independent apparel brand operating at aoktshirts.com. We design and sell print-on-demand apparel through platforms including Etsy and other e-commerce providers.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate our business:

  • Information You Provide: Name and email address (e.g., when contacting us), and order-related information when purchasing through third-party platforms.
  • Automatically Collected Data: Basic web server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) for performance and security.
  • Third-Party Platforms: When you interact with our products through third-party services (e.g., Etsy, Pinterest), those platforms may collect and process your data according to their own privacy policies. We do not control or store personal data collected directly by these platforms unless you contact us separately.

Pinterest API

AOK T-Shirts uses the Pinterest API to manage and schedule pins related to our product collections. This integration accesses only our own Pinterest business account, does not store or process Pinterest user data, and is used solely for content publication and analytics of our own account. Pinterest's own privacy policy governs your use of Pinterest.com.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • Monitor and improve website performance
  • Fulfill orders through third-party fulfillment providers
  • Manage content and social media presence via authorized platform APIs
  • Comply with legal obligations

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Where applicable, we process personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Legitimate interest — responding to inquiries, maintaining website security
  • Consent — when you voluntarily contact us
  • Contractual necessity — fulfilling orders through third-party platforms

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We only share limited data when necessary with:

  • Etsy — our primary storefront platform
  • Printful — order fulfillment (name and shipping address shared only for order delivery)
  • Pinterest — social commerce and product discovery
  • Service providers required to operate our business

Each third party operates under its own privacy policy.

Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose it was collected for or to comply with legal or operational requirements. When no longer needed, data is deleted or securely discarded.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you, object to or restrict certain processing, or withdraw consent where applicable. To make a request, contact us at info@aoktshirts.com.

Cookies & Tracking

We currently do not use behavioral advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or retargeting scripts. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

This website is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
